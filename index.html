Spot the Disinformation

Can you identify the signs of AI manipulation? Review these common social media scenarios, then toggle the scanner to see the breakdown.

Scenario 1: The "Panic" Bot

PatriotVoice2026 @TrueNews_8832 • 2m

URGENT: Just saw military convoys moving into the city center! They are preparing to shut down the election! 🚨 The mainstream media is completely silent about this! We must take action NOW before it's too late! Wake up!

❌ Generic Handle: "PatriotVoice" + random numbers (8832) is a hallmark of mass-generated bot accounts.
❌ Algorithmic Trigger Words: Words like "URGENT", "SILENT", and "NOW" are programmed to bypass critical thinking and trigger fear.
❌ Lack of Evidence: Makes a massive claim (military convoys) with zero photos or verificable sources.

Scenario 2: The "Deepfake" Giveaway

Elon_Giveaway_Official @Elon_X_Free • 15m
❌ Lip-Sync Error: AI video generation often fails to perfectly match lip movements with the audio track.
❌ "Urgency" Trap: "Next 30 minutes" creates artificial pressure so you don't double-check the facts.
❌ The Promise: No legitimate public figure conducts financial giveaways via wallet deposits. This is a classic scam script.
